Why Space Tourism is the Next Big Thing in Tech

In the past few decades, space exploration was reserved for governments and large space agencies, but in recent years, a new frontier has emerged—space tourism. The idea of traveling beyond Earth's atmosphere to experience weightlessness, see Earth from space, and even catch a glimpse of distant planets has captivated the imaginations of millions. With the rapid advancements in space technology and the involvement of private companies like SpaceX, Blue Origin, and Virgin Galactic, space tourism is quickly becoming a reality.
